# ZenHabit AI - Smart Habit Tracker

ZenHabit AI is a sophisticated mobile-first web application designed to help individuals build and maintain sustainable healthy habits through the power of Behavioral Science and Artificial Intelligence.

## 🌟 Key Features

### 🤖 AI-Powered Insights
- **Behavior Analysis:** Uses Google's Gemini AI to analyze your consistency patterns.
- **Adaptive Recommendations:** The system suggests increasing difficulty when you are consistent or reducing it if you are struggling (Reinforcement Learning principles).
- **Personalized Coaching:** Get specific feedback on your completion rates and frequency.

### 🧩 Habit Management
- **Versatile Tracking:** Track anything from water intake (liters) and reading (minutes) to prayer and Quran recitation.
- **Goal Setting:** Set personalized targets, units, and frequencies (daily, weekly, etc.).
- **Smart Logging:** One-tap completion logging directly from your dashboard.

### 📊 Progress Visualization
- **Interactive Charts:** Visualize your weekly consistency with clean, responsive charts.
- **Points & Badges:** Stay motivated through a rewarding gamification system. Earn XP for every habit completed.
- **Mobile-First UX:** A sleek, gesture-friendly interface designed to feel like a native app.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** React 19, Vite, TypeScript
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS (v4), Framer Motion
- **Backend/Database:** Firebase Authentication & Firestore
- **AI Engine:** Google Gemini AI (via `@google/genai`)
- **Visuals:** Recharts, Lucide Icons

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. **Sign In:** Use your Google account to create a secure personal profile.
2. **Create a Habit:** Tap the "Add Habit" button to define your first goal (e.g., "Read", "Exercise").
3. **Log Daily:** Tap the checkmark icon every time you complete your habit to earn points.
4. **Get Insights:** Tap on any habit to see your progress chart and AI-generated coaching advice.

## 🔐 Security & Privacy
- **End-to-End Auth:** Managed by Firebase for world-class security.
- **Data Scoping:** Strict Firestore Security Rules ensure only you can access your habit logs and profile data.
